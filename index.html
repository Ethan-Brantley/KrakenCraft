KrakenCraft is an exclusive Minecraft Server with a small player base dedicated to maintaining a fun, friendly community. Plugins are carefully curated by a team of passionate individuals to ensure intuitive gameplay and an experience faithful to the original game. KrakenCraft players are carefully vetted. The server contains a variety of worlds ranging from vanilla survival, worlds with an overarching storyline, to a growing number of popular team-based games (i.e. BedWars, VillageDefense, SheepQuest, and more).

If you’re reading this, you most likely have already received an invite from a current player. We’re excited to have you join us!

Lobby

KrakenCraft Mining Corp™ has staked a claim on the rogue asteroid 1342-C. This asteroid is rich in the purple mineral Purpur-92 but during mining operations it was also discovered that this particular asteroid is also the spawning grounds of a majestic, yet dangerous creature, the Space Kraken. Fortunately measures were enacted to keep the Kraken contained and the mining operations can continue...For now.

Coincidentally, this asteroid is also the location of many portals that lead to other worlds that are waiting to be explored.

Narrative Survival

A Minecraft world with a progressive storyline. Each player creates their story as they play along. Their conversations, actions, and choices build their unique tale and influence the narrative as a whole. The story is divided up into chapters and books as major events develop. Without plotting out details, basic structure may be discussed ahead of time and external elements introduced to keep events interesting and moving along.

The Cloud Project

Classic Survival with a goal: In the cloud project, our collective goal is to build out the main island to include preservation habitats for every biome and mob in the game!
