Welcome to Krakencraft.

Begin Descent.

KrakenCraft is an exclusive Minecraft Server with a small player base dedicated to maintaining a fun, friendly community. Plugins are carefully curated by a team of passionate individuals to ensure intuitive gameplay and an experience faithful to the original game. KrakenCraft players are carefully vetted. The server contains a variety of worlds ranging from vanilla survival, worlds with an overarching storyline, to a growing number of popular team-based games (i.e. BedWars, VillageDefense, SheepQuest, and more).

If you’re reading this, you most likely have already received an invite from a current player. We’re excited to have you join us!
