KrakenCraft Narrative Survival

A Minecraft world with a progressive storyline. Each player creates their story as they play along. Their conversations, actions, and choices build their unique tale and influence the narrative as a whole. The story is divided up into chapters and books as major events develop. Without plotting out details, basic structure may be discussed ahead of time and external elements introduced to keep events interesting and moving along.
